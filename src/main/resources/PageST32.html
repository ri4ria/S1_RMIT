Outcome 1

Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander people enjoy long and healthy lives.

Long-Term Health Conditions

Contains a count of persons reporting various types of long-term health conditions for Aboriginal and/or Torres Strait Islander people.

Outcome 5

Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander students achieve their full learning potential.

Highest Year of School Completed

Contains data for the highest year of school completed by Indigenous status and sex. Tracks persons aged 15 years and older who are no longer in secondary school by year level.

Outcome 8

Strong economic participation and development of Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander people and communities.

Indigenous Status

Tracks the number of people in the following categories:

  • Self-identified as Aboriginal and/or Torres Strait Islander people
  • Self-identified as non-Indigenous
  • Did not state their Indigenous status

Total Weekly Household Income

Contains data based on total household weekly income by Indigenous status of the household.
