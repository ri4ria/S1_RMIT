Addressing 'Closing the Gap'

Our approach to addressing this social challenge includes raiding awareness of all 17 target outcomes, however we will be particularly focusing on three: outcomes 1, 5, and 8.

TThis website provides data on these outcomes, which you can use to compare between two years: 2016 and 2021.

We have also provided additional resources and additional insights on how progress is tracking on the targets of the 'Closing the Gap' campaign.

How to Use Our Website

A majority of site activity will be present in the 'Data Analyses' tab. The results that appear depend on what target outcome and type of data you are interested in.

Generally, you must choose your filter options in the sidebar.
