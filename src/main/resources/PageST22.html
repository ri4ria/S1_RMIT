Changes between 2016 and 2021 Censuses

On this page, you can find the difference between 2016 and 2021 datasets.
Three datasets are available to compare:

Highest Year of School Completed

Contains data for the highest year of school completed by Indigenous status and sex. Tracks persons age 15 years and older who are no longer attending secondary school (by year level).

Indigenous Status

Tracks the number of people in the following categories: self-identified as Aboriginal and/or Torres Strait Islander; self-identified as non-Indigenous; or, did not state their Indigenous status.

Total Weekly Household Income

Contains data based on total household weekly income by Indigenous status of the household.

These datasets are from the 2016 census and 2021 census, from the Australian Bureau of Statistics.
For more information, visit the data source.
